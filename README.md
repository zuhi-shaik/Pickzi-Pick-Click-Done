Pickzi is an online shopping website where the users can search and purchase products of men,women and kids.
Users can:

- Browse and explore different products. 
- Add products to their cart or wishlist.  
- Sign up, log in, and manage their account.  
- Place orders.
- Users can clearify their queries with pickzi assistant chatbot.  
- Save their shipping address for easy checkout.

The website is designed to give a smooth and easy shopping experience. 
It is mobile-friendly and works on both phones and computers.  
Frontend is built with React and backend with Node.js/Express.  

live link : https://pickzi-pick-click-done.vercel.app/
